Gifting Discord Nitro is a great way to show your appreciation!

Most Preferred

Option 1: Directly in a DM

Gifting directly in a DM is the easiest way to send Nitro and allows you to completely customize your gift link:

  1. Push the button below to open Momo's profile and select Send Message.
  2. Click the Gift Icon () located at the side of the Discord text box.
  3. Select the plan you'd like to send and optionally customize the gift.
  4. Complete your purchase and a Nitro subscription will be delivered to Momo!
OPEN PROFILE

Option 2: From the settings menu

If you prefer to generate and send a standalone URL voucher, follow these steps:

  1. Navigate to your User Settings and select the Nitro tab.
  2. Choose Gift Nitro, select the $9.99 plan and complete checkout.
  3. Discord will generate a discord.gift/xxxx link. Send this link to Momo in a DM.

Option 3: Digital gift card

Supporters residing within the United States or EU who wish to use other payment methods via external digital retailers:

  1. Push the button below to see the Discord Nitro gift card on Amazon, or navigate to your favorite digital retailer.
  2. Choose how many months of Nitro you want to gift and complete checkout to purchase the digital code.
  3. Amazon will deliver a digital voucher code to your email; or if using a different retailer, follow their instructions to receive the code.
  4. Copy this gift code and send it directly to Momo in a DM.
